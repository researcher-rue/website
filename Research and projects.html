Schizophrenia

Schizophrenia is characterised by subtle premorbid cognitive abnormalities that present years before the onset of psychosis in adulthood. Cognitive functions found to be impaired in both adults with schizophrenia and children at high risk of developing schizophrenia include both early and later neural responses elicited by errors. As certain cognitive abnormalities follow distinct developmental trajectories, this project seeks to characterise the developmental trajectory of early and later neural response abnormalities elicited by errors among youth with different risk profiles for schizophrenia.
