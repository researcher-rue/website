Publications and Projects

Master of Philosophy Project

Schizophrenia is characterised by subtle premorbid cognitive abnormalities that emerge years before the onset of psychosis in adulthood. One such abnormality observed in at risk youth is altered amplitude in electroencephalographic event related potentials (ERPs) elicited following erroneous behaviour. These ERPs include the error related negativity (ERN/Ne) and the later response to errors, known as error positivity (Pe).

This project focuses on mapping how abnormalities in ERN/Ne and Pe amplitudes develop from the premorbid phase into the early prodromal phase.
